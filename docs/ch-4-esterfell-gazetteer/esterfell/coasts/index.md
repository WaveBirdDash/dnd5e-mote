# Coasts of Esterfell

## Strait of Shallows

The **Strait of Shallows** is a channel dividing [Lenya](../lenya/index.md) from [Malagwa](../malagwa.md) and [Barria](../barria.md) along the western side of [Esterfell](../index.md). Ships of the [Esterfell Accord](../../../ch-2-people-of-mote/societies/esterfell-accord/index.md) use the Strait for commerce between [Perlipont](../../../ch-2-people-of-mote/societies/esterfell-accord/perlipont.md) and [Barrinview](../../../ch-2-people-of-mote/societies/esterfell-accord/barrinview.md) in order to avoid taking the more treacherous paths of the [Attalya Mountains](../lenya/attalya-mountains/index.md) by foot or horse.
