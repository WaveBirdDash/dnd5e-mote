# Taltol Peak

**Taltol Peak** is the highest point of elevation in [Esterfell](../../../index.md) and is the southernmost peak of the [Attalya Mountains](../index.md). Waters from the [Roiling River](roiling-river.md) spiral down from the summit to feed the [Esterfell Sea](../../esterfell-sea/index.md).

## History

The mountain's name is Elvish for "big toe".
