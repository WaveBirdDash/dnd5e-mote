# Bridgeport

**Bridgeport** is a town on [Westin Island](../../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/esterfell-sea/westin-island/index.md) in the [Esterfell Sea](../../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/esterfell-sea/index.md). It marks a midpoint for foot travel across the [Bridge of Commerce](../road-of-commerce.md) from [Gracia](../gracia.md), then south through [Ashín](../ashin/index.md) toward [Tillë](../tille.md), and is also a busy port for travel by ship across the western and northern regions of the Sea.
