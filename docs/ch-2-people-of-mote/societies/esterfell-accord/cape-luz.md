# Cape Luz

**Cape Luz** is a harbor town on the northern coast of [Lenya](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/index.md). The [Road of Commerce](road-of-commerce.md) connects Cape Luz to [Suizenloch](suizenloch/index.md) to the west, [Cape Bec](cape-bec/index.md) to the east, and [Norgrad](norgrad.md) to the south.
