# Tillë

**Tillë** is a town on the west coast of the [Esterfell Sea](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/esterfell-sea/index.md) sitting at the base of [Tillë Peak](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/attalya-mountains/tille-peak/index.md). Travelers can reach [Zutto](zutto/index.md) and [Salkinar](salkinar.md) by ship routes, while the [Road of Commerce](road-of-commerce.md) connects Tillë to [Ashín](ashin/index.md) via bridge to the north and [Needledale](needledale.md) to the south.
