# Gracia

**Gracia** is a town on the west coast of [Esterfell Sea](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/esterfell-sea/index.md). The [Road of Commerce](road-of-commerce.md) connects Gracia to [Norgrad](norgrad.md) to the north, [Sunderin](sunderin.md) through [Attalya Mountains](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/attalya-mountains), and [Bridgeport](bridgeport/index.md) on [Westin Island](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/esterfell-sea/westin-island/index.md).
