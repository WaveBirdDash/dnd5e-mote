# Alyseris

**Alyseris** is an elf mage currently working with [The Commune](../).

## Information

### Mental

- pronouns: she/her
- languages: Common, Draconic, Elvish

### Physical

- species: half-elf
- class: Sorcerer (Draconic Sorcery)

## History

### Childhood

Alyseris was born next to a base of a waterfall at a rushing river during a thunderstorm.

She had to learn to walk, swim, climb, and survive the elements at a young age.

### Adulthood

Alyseris has an impressive ability to swim and control weather, with an innate imperviousness to the effects of weather such as lightning.

Her driving goal is to find other storm-born mages to better learn her place in the world.

## Personality

Due to her elemental origins, Alyseris is most comfortable in natural settings.
