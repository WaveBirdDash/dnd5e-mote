# The Guild of Associated Arcartisans

**The Guild of Associated Arcartisans**, also known as **The Arcartisans**, is a conglomeration of crafter merchants studying the potential power of domestic magic in partnership with [Balut's Academerie for the Magistically Inclinated](../baluts-academerie/index.md). The Arcartisans operate out of [Bridgeport](../../societies/esterfell-accord/bridgeport/index.md).
